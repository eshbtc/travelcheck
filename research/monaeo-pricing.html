FAQs

Is there a free trial for Monaeo?

Yes. Monaeo Personal offers a free trial so you can test features like location tracking and audit reporting before committing to a paid plan.

What’s included in a Monaeo subscription?

All Monaeo plans include automatic location tracking, real-time alerts for tax residency thresholds, and downloadable audit-ready reports. Annual and Enterprise plans also come with additional features and support.

Are there any hidden fees with Monaeo?

No. Monaeo pricing is transparent—there are no setup fees, hidden charges, or surprise add-ons.

Do I need to enter my location manually?

Nope! Monaeo automatically tracks your location in the background using your device’s GPS—no manual input or check-ins required.

Does Monaeo offer pricing for businesses or teams?

Yes. Monaeo offers custom enterprise pricing for organizations managing global mobility, business travel, or multi-state payroll compliance. This includes dedicated support, API access, and employee-level tracking.

What happens after the free trial?

At the end of your free trial, you can choose a monthly or annual plan to continue uninterrupted access. If you don’t upgrade, your location tracking will stop, but you’ll still have access to your saved data.

Can I export reports for my accountant?

Yes. You can easily generate and export audit-ready reports to share with your accountant or tax advisor—great for proving state residency or preparing for an audit.
